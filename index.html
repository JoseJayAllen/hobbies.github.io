Photography

One of my biggest passions is photography. I love capturing moments in time, whether it's a beautiful sunset, a funny expression on someone's face, or the intricate details of a flower. I enjoy exploring different styles of photography, from landscape and portrait to macro and street photography. I find it incredibly rewarding to learn new techniques and experiment with different settings on my camera. It's like a puzzle, trying to get the perfect combination of light, composition, and timing to create a stunning image.

I also love the feeling of being present in the moment when I'm taking photos. It forces me to slow down, pay attention to my surroundings, and appreciate the beauty in everyday things. I'm always looking for new and interesting subjects to photograph, and I love sharing my work with others.

Photography is more than just a hobby for me; it's a way of expressing myself creatively and connecting with the world around me. It's a journey of learning, exploration, and discovery, and I'm excited to see where it takes me next.

Travelling

Traveling is another one of my favorite things to do. I love exploring new places, experiencing different cultures, and learning about the world around me. It's like opening a window to a whole new perspective, and I find it incredibly enriching.

I've been fortunate enough to travel to some amazing places in the Philippines, including:

  • Baler, Aurora
  • Vigan, Ilocos Sur
  • San Juan, La Union
  • Pangasinan
  • Bohol
Mobile Gaming

Mobile gaming is another big part of my life. I love the convenience of being able to play games anytime, anywhere, right on my phone. I enjoy a wide variety of genres, from strategy and puzzle games to action and RPGs.

My gaming interests span across a variety of platforms and genres, but three titles stand out: Mobile Legends: Bang Bang, Call of Duty: Mobile, and Roblox Dress to Impress. Each offers a unique experience and caters to different aspects of my gaming personality.

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang is my go-to for competitive action. The fast-paced, team-based gameplay keeps me on my toes, constantly strategizing and adapting to changing situations. The diverse roster of heroes, each with unique abilities and playstyles, offers endless possibilities for experimentation and mastery. I enjoy the thrill of leading my team to victory, and the satisfaction of outsmarting my opponents.

Call of Duty: Mobile provides a more tactical and immersive experience. The realistic graphics, diverse maps, and variety of game modes, from team deathmatch to battle royale, keep me engaged for hours. I appreciate the skill-based gameplay, where precision and strategy are key to success. It's a game where I can test my reflexes, hone my aim, and truly feel like a soldier on the battlefield.

Roblox Dress to Impress, on the other hand, offers a creative outlet for my fashion sense. While not a traditional "game" in the same sense as the others, it allows me to express myself through virtual fashion. I enjoy creating unique outfits, experimenting with different styles, and showcasing my creativity. It's a fun and lighthearted way to relax and explore my artistic side.

Listening to Music
Music is my lifeblood! It's a constant companion, a mood booster, and a source of endless inspiration. I can get lost in a world of melodies and lyrics for hours on end, and I'm always on the lookout for new sounds and artists to discover. Speaking of discovering new music, Spotify is my absolute haven! I love the endless possibilities of its playlists, from curated genre selections to personalized recommendations. I can easily spend a whole day hopping between different playlists, exploring new artists, and rediscovering old favorites. My current obsession is BINI, the nation's girl group! Their music is so catchy, energetic, and empowering. I love their unique style, their powerful vocals, and the way they always bring a positive vibe to their music. It's no exaggeration to say that I'm completely addicted to their songs. Beyond BINI, my musical taste is pretty eclectic.
  • I love the chill vibes of Cup of Joe
  • the nostalgic charm of Maki
  • the indie pop brilliance of Rex Orange County
  • the soulful melodies of The Ridleys
  • the raw energy of Paramore
Each artist brings something unique and special to my musical journey, and I'm always grateful for the emotional connection that music provides.
Watching Movies/Series

My love for movies and series is a constant in my life. I find myself drawn to a variety of genres, each offering a unique escape and a chance to immerse myself in captivating stories.

The Marvel Cinematic Universe holds a special place in my heart. I'm a huge fan of the interconnected storylines, the epic action sequences, and the memorable characters. From the adventures of Iron Man and Captain America to the cosmic journeys of Thor and Guardians of the Galaxy, I've been hooked on every Marvel movie and series.

The Walking Dead series has also been a major obsession. I love the post-apocalyptic setting, the complex characters, and the constant struggle for survival. The show's ability to blend horror, drama, and action keeps me on the edge of my seat, and I'm always eager to see what happens next.

Young Sheldon is a delightful series that brings a heartwarming and humorous perspective on the early life of a young genius. It's a show that I can always count on for a good laugh and a reminder of the simple joys of childhood.

And of course, I can't forget about my Netflix addiction! I love browsing through the endless library of movies and shows, discovering hidden gems, and getting lost in new stories. From documentaries to dramas to reality shows, Netflix has something for everyone, and I'm always happy to spend hours exploring its vast library.

Dancing

Dancing has been a significant part of my life ever since I joined a dance group in high school. It became a passion that brought me immense joy and fulfillment. The hours spent practicing and perfecting routines with my fellow dancers were not only filled with hard work but also with moments of laughter, camaraderie, and shared dedication to our craft.

Competing in various competitions, whether at barangay events or school programs, was both nerve-wracking and exhilarating. The rush of adrenaline before stepping onto the stage, the energy of the crowd, and the sense of accomplishment after each performance fueled my love for dance even more. These experiences taught me valuable lessons about teamwork, perseverance, and the importance of believing in oneself.

Although my high school days are behind me, my love for dancing continues to thrive. It has become a form of self-expression, a way to connect with others, and a source of pure joy. Exploring different dance styles, moving to the rhythm of the music, and feeling the freedom that comes with dancing are moments that I cherish deeply.

Dance has a way of bringing people together, transcending boundaries, and fostering a sense of unity. It is a universal language that speaks to the soul and allows me to communicate my emotions without words. So, if you ever catch me dancing, know that it is not just a hobby but a heartfelt passion that brings light and energy into my life.

Spending Money

Okay, so I love food! I'm always hungry, and I'm not afraid to admit it. I could eat all day long, and I'm always up for trying new things.

I also love to shop, especially online! I spend a lot of time browsing TikTok and Shopee, looking for the latest trends and deals. I'm always buying things I don't really need, but that's okay. It makes me happy! I love getting packages in the mail, and it's always exciting to see what's inside.

I know it's not the best habit, but it's just so much fun! I'm trying to be more mindful of my spending, but it's hard when there are so many tempting things out there.

Hiking

Hiking is my happy place! There's something about being out in nature, surrounded by mountains and hills, that just makes me feel so alive.

La Trinidad is such a perfect place for hiking, with so many beautiful trails to explore. I love the feeling of fresh air in my lungs, the sound of birds singing, and the view from the top of a mountain. It's a great way to get exercise, clear my head, and connect with nature.

I love the challenge of a good hike, pushing myself to reach new heights and discover new trails. It's a great way to get out of my comfort zone and experience something new.
